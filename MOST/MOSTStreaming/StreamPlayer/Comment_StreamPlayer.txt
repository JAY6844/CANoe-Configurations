
MOST Streaming: Stream Player
**************************

With this configuration you can record up to 60
synchronous MOST channels to hard disk.
Note:
Recording 60 channels at 48 KHz for one
hour requires about 12 GByte free disk space.

The StreamPlayer panel also lets you replay
recorded audio data. For this purpose select 2 or
4 channels in the allocation table and press play.

Find a detailed documentation here:
\MOST\MOSTStreaming\Documentation\MOST_SyncStreaming_Documentation.pdf
